Rally Twitch Bot

You must have both a twitch and rally account to register

Registration Steps

  1. Click the button below.
  2. Click the login with Rally button on the next page
  3. Log in with Rally (read note below)
  4. Authorize the bot
  5. Click the Twitch login button
  6. Log in to twitch (make sure you are logged in with the correct account)
  7. Authorize the bot
  8. Follow the bot on twitch
  9. Continue to kill it at life

Note: Sometimes the Rally authorization site will
redirect you to the main rally login page.
If this happens come back to this page and start over.
It will not work if you login on the rally.io page.
