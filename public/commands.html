Rally Twitch Bot

Commands

  • $send: Sends coins to another user
    $send {Twitch Username} {amount} {type of coin}
    Example: $send d4rkcide 10 RLY

  • $donate/$tip: Donate and tip do the exact same thing, send coins to the coin's creator
    $donate/$tip {amount} {type of coin}
    Example: $donate/$tip 5.5 WUX

    • Note: For the commands $tip, $donate, and $send if you add usd to the end of the command you can send the amount input in usd instead of coins
    For example, $send d4rkcide 10 PLAY will send d4rkcide 10 PLAY coins
    $send d4rkcide 10 PLAY usd will send d4rkcide $10 worth of PLAY coins.

  • $airdrop: Sends coins to the last x number of chatters in twitch chat
    $airdrop {amount} {type of coin} {number of people to airdrop on (max 10)}
    Example: $airdrop 25 DTQ 5 (each person gets 5 DTQ)

  • $coin: Send a message to twitch chat with a link to the bot registration site
    $coin
    Example: $coin

  • $bot: blacklists bots so they do not get included in airdrops
    $bot {bot name} {bot name}
    Example: $bot nightbot awesomebot streamelements someotherbot

  • $commands: Sends a message in chat with a link to this page
    $commands
    Example: $commands
